1. Introduction

This Employee Handbook outlines the core HR policies, workplace expectations, and employment guidelines for all employees of Senurukz Solutions.

It applies to:

Full-time employees
Contract employees (where applicable by agreement)
Remote and hybrid workers
Global offices unless overridden by local labor law

The purpose of this handbook is to ensure consistency, fairness, and operational clarity across all regions where Senurukz Solutions operates.


2. Employment Structure

Senurukz Solutions follows a structured employment model consisting of:

Full-time permanent employees
Fixed-term contractors
Internship and graduate programs
Remote-first distributed engineering teams

All employment relationships are governed by formal contracts and applicable local labor regulations.


3. Working Hours & Flexibility

Standard Working Hours
Core hours: 09:30 to 17:30 (local office time)
Workweek: Monday to Friday
Total weekly hours: ~40 hours (standard guideline)
Flexible Working Policy

Senurukz Solutions supports a flexible work structure:

Flexible start window: 08:00 to 10:30
Flexible end time based on completion of core hours
Employees are expected to maintain minimum daily availability overlap of 4 hours with their team
Remote Work Policy
Eligible employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week
Engineering, AI, and product teams may adopt fully remote arrangements with manager approval
Certain roles (e.g., infrastructure, security operations) may require higher on-site presence
4. Leave Policy

Senurukz Solutions provides a comprehensive leave structure designed to support employee well-being.

Annual Vacation Leave
Employees receive 22 vacation days per year
Leave is accrued monthly at approximately 1.83 days per month
Vacation can be used for personal travel, rest, or personal obligations
Sick Leave
Employees are entitled to 10 paid sick leave days per year
Medical documentation may be required for absences exceeding 2 consecutive days
Carry Forward Policy
Up to 6 unused vacation days may be carried into the next calendar year
Carry-forward leave must be used within the first 6 months of the new year


5. Additional Leave Types

Senurukz Solutions also provides specialized leave categories:

Parental Leave
Primary caregiver: up to 16 weeks paid leave
Secondary caregiver: up to 6 weeks paid leave
Bereavement Leave
Up to 5 paid days for immediate family members
Marriage Leave
5 paid days for employee marriage
Study / Training Leave
Up to 7 paid days annually for professional certifications or academic programs


6. Probation Policy

All new employees are subject to a probationary period:

Standard probation duration: 3 months
Extension: up to 1 additional month if performance evaluation is inconclusive

During probation:

Employees receive full salary and benefits
Performance is evaluated based on role-specific KPIs
Confirmation requires approval from direct manager and HR


7. Performance Evaluation

Senurukz Solutions conducts structured performance reviews:

Quarterly performance check-ins
Annual performance appraisal cycle
Continuous feedback model supported by team leads

Performance is evaluated based on:

Technical or functional contribution
Collaboration and teamwork
Delivery consistency
Innovation and problem-solving ability


8. Code of Conduct

Employees are expected to maintain professional standards including:

Ethical behavior in all business interactions
Respectful communication across teams and cultures
Protection of company and client data
Compliance with security and confidentiality requirements

Violations of conduct policies may result in disciplinary action, including termination.


9. Hybrid Work Expectations

Employees working remotely must ensure:

Stable internet connectivity during working hours
Availability during core collaboration hours
Participation in scheduled meetings
Secure handling of company systems and data

All work must comply with Senurukz cybersecurity and data protection policies.


10. Compensation Principles (High-Level)

While detailed salary structures are maintained separately, Senurukz Solutions follows:

Market-aligned compensation benchmarks
Performance-based increments
Annual bonus eligibility for eligible employees
Equity or long-term incentive programs for senior roles


11. Learning & Development

Senurukz Solutions actively supports employee growth:

Annual learning budget per employee
Access to technical certification programs
Internal knowledge-sharing sessions
Leadership development tracks for senior staff

Employees are encouraged to dedicate time to continuous learning during working hours.


12. Health, Safety & Well-being

The company promotes employee well-being through:

Mental health support resources
Flexible scheduling during high workload periods
Ergonomic work environment guidelines
Global employee assistance program (EAP)


13. Information Security Policy (Summary)

Employees must:

Use approved company systems only
Protect login credentials and access keys
Avoid unauthorized data sharing
Follow least-privilege access principles
Report security incidents immediately

Security is treated as a core engineering requirement, not an optional guideline.


14. Disciplinary Policy

Senurukz Solutions maintains a structured disciplinary framework:

Verbal warning
Written warning
Final warning
Termination (if required)

Serious violations (e.g., data breaches, fraud, misconduct) may bypass progressive steps.


15. Policy Updates

This handbook is reviewed:

Annually, or
When required due to legal, operational, or organizational changes

Employees will be notified of all significant updates through official HR communication channels.


16. Summary

The Senurukz Solutions Employee Handbook defines a flexible, performance-driven, and globally aligned workplace structure designed to support innovation, productivity, and employee well-being.

The company emphasizes:

Trust-based remote work
Balanced work-life structure
Strong performance culture
Continuous learning
High standards of professionalism and security
