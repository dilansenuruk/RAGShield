1. Overview

Senurukz Solutions maintains a hybrid global holiday calendar to support its distributed workforce across North America, Europe, Asia-Pacific, and the Middle East.

The company observes a combination of:

National public holidays (by office location)
Global company-wide shutdown days
Optional cultural observance days
Flexible floating holidays for employees

All primary offices remain closed on officially declared company holidays unless otherwise specified by executive notice.
Regional HR teams may publish additional localized holidays based on jurisdictional requirements and labor laws.

7. Company-Wide Floating Holidays Policy

Annual entitlement: 4 floating days per employee
Can be used for:

Approval is managed through HR self-service portal.


8. Special Company Events (Non-Working Days)

9. Regional Office Closure Policy

Offices may close early on the day before major holidays (half-day closure).
Customer support maintains reduced staffing only for critical enterprise clients during global shutdown periods.
Engineering teams may observe on-call rotations during system maintenance windows.


10. HR Compliance Notes

All holiday schedules comply with local labor laws and statutory requirements.
In case of conflict between company-wide and local holidays, local statutory law takes precedence.
HR reserves the right to update the calendar based on regulatory changes or business continuity needs.


11. Summary

The Senurukz Solutions 2026 holiday calendar is designed to balance:

Global operational consistency
Regional legal compliance
Employee well-being and cultural inclusivity
System maintenance and engineering downtime

The company maintains a structured but flexible holiday framework to support its distributed global workforce while ensuring enterprise service continuity.
