1. Company Introduction

Senurukz Solutions is an enterprise technology company specializing in cloud-based software systems, artificial intelligence applications, and enterprise data infrastructure platforms. The company builds scalable solutions that help organizations transform raw operational data into actionable intelligence, enabling faster and more accurate decision-making across business functions.

Founded in 2019, Senurukz Solutions has grown from a small engineering-focused startup into a mid-sized enterprise software provider serving clients across North America, Europe, and the Asia-Pacific region.

The company operates primarily in the B2B SaaS (Software-as-a-Service) and enterprise consulting technology sectors, with a strong emphasis on AI-driven automation, analytics platforms, and cloud-native architecture design.
